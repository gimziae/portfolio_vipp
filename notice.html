Where is the Vipp Hotel located? 호출
New rooms are always in the making at the Vipp Hotel. So far, we have three rooms available. Get out of the city and into nature at the 55 m2 Vipp shelter at lake Immeln in Sweden, or go for maximized urbanity in the 400 m2 Vipp loft in Copenhagen. Or sleep under a towering chimney in a rebuilt water pump station at the Vipp Chimney House, north of Copenhagen. Latest addition to our hotel concept is a Farmhouse in Lolland from 1775 fully equipped with modern convenience.
How do I get to the Vipp Hotel from the airport?
Vipp Shelter: If you are going to the shelter, we recommend you to fly to Malmö and rent a car at the airport and drive to the shelter. It is a drive of approx. 127 km. If you fly to Copenhagen the drive will be approx. 150 km.
Vipp Loft: From Copenhagen Airport to the Vipp loft is 15 minutes by car or 15 minutes by metro followed by a 10-minute walk. Vipp Chimney House: From Copenhagen Central Station, take the S-train to Svanemøllen station followed by a 10-minute walk. Vipp Farmhouse: If you fly to Copenhagen the drive will be approx. 150 km / 2 hours. When you have booked one of our rooms, we will be in touch to provide you with directions and other practical information for your stay at the Vipp Hotel.
