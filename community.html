FAQ

FAQ

Where is the Vipp Hotel located?

New rooms are always in the making at the Vipp Hotel. So far, we have three rooms available. Get out of the city and into nature at the 55 m2 Vipp shelter at lake Immeln in Sweden, or go for maximized urbanity in the 400 m2 Vipp loft in Copenhagen. Or sleep under a towering chimney in a rebuilt water pump station at the Vipp Chimney House, north of Copenhagen. Latest addition to our hotel concept is a Farmhouse in Lolland from 1775 fully equipped with modern convenience. Explore all the rooms here.

How many people can stay in your rooms?

All rooms in the Vipp Hotel are for adults over the age of 16 only, no children and no pets.
Vipp Shelter: max 2 adults, one queen size bed. Vipp Loft: max 4 adults, two queen size beds. Vipp Chimney House: max 4 adults, two queen size beds. Vipp Farmhouse: max 6 adults, three double beds.

What is included in a stay?

When you stay at the Vipp Hotel, you are welcomed by a location with all the necessities. The refrigerator holds cold refreshments and tea and coffee are ready to be brewed.

Do you have room service?

We do not offer room service or a restaurant, but there is a fully equipped kitchen just waiting to be used. Information on nearby grocery stores, cafés and restaurants will be sent to you prior to your stay.

What is your cancellation policy?

All bookings at the Vipp Hotel are non-refundable. If you cannot make it for your booked reservation, we do offer the option of changing the date of your stay according to availability. A second cancellation cannot be refunded or rescheduled.

Is parking available?

Yes, there is street parking just outside the Vipp Loft, free parking by the Vipp Shelter and a private parking spot at the Vipp Chimney House and Vipp Farmhouse.

Do you have WiFi?

Free WiFi is available.

How do I pay?

Full pre-payment by credit card or bank transfer at the time of reservation. A valid credit card is required to confirm your booking.
