As soon as you check in at

The Vipp Hotel is not a hotel in the traditional sense.
Instead of having many rooms in one location, we
offer unique rooms at various destinations.

An untraditional hotel

Our rooms are spread out at various lacations, so we do not
have a reception or a concierge. You won't find a 24-hour
lobby, a gym, daily housekiiping or fitness facilities.

Fully Furnished

At the Vipp Hotel, we invite you to experience our philosophy
of design in placss out of the ordinary.our hotel is not like many others.
Instead of having all our rooms in one place, we offer a curaled
selection of destination for unique experiences.
